About Us

Denali Building Improvement (DBI) is a general contractor dedicated to servicing residential and commercial spaces in the beautiful Marin County. Our team specializes in an efficient array of services including remodeling, additions, extensions, and restorations. From bathrooms and kitchens to our relaxing custom indoor-outdoor saunas and steam rooms, DBI values transparency, consistent communication, and collaboration with our clients to ensure a space that is uniquely tailored to them.
